THE EDGE
Sovereign Entry Protocol — Practitioner Reference

What This System Is

The Edge reads three languages simultaneously — mathematical, archetypal, and somatic — and treats them as dialects of the same underlying signal. The temporal field has a quality in every moment. The chaos metrics measure it mathematically. The archetypes name its psychological character. Your body feels it directly.

When all three agree, you have an edge. When they diverge, you have information.

The decision tree exists not to replace your judgment but to structure the conversation your nervous system is already having.

Before You Begin — The Intention Ritual

Not a scored layer. A calibration.

Before scoring begins, establish your felt reference state. What does a hell-yes trade feel like in the body? Not excitement — ease. Warm, expansive, unhurried. Visualize money flowing in without urgency. Hold that feeling. Everything that follows is measured against it.

If you can't locate that feeling clearly, your instrument may not be clean. Flag it and revisit.

The Hierarchy

Stated: Somatic → Technical → Fundamental → Astrological timing

Actual: The somatic instrument is the primary instrument. All analytical layers are preparation to trust it. The body is not a tiebreaker — it may be the actual signal.

Clean signal baseline: Calm ease. Belly warmth, openness, absence of urgency. Not activated, not excited — settled.

Contamination protocol: Personal life activation (stress, sleep disruption, significant emotional events) creates ambient nervous system noise that corrupts market reads. If you can't locate calm ease as a baseline, flag the session and revisit when the instrument is clean.

The Pre-Filter

Layer 1 — The Room (Macro Conditions)
What's the vibe?

The opening read. Before looking at any specific instrument — what is the ambient quality of this moment? Markets have texture. Some days are alive with directed movement. Some are murky with pending news. Some are actively hostile.

Resonant +1

Macro environment supportive. No major catalysts in the immediate window. Market moving with coherent energy.

Murky 0

Something is pending or unclear. Elevated event risk approaching — scheduled Fed, CPI, NFP, earnings within your hold window. Regime ambiguous. Proceed with awareness.

Disrupted STOP

Major scheduled risk event imminent, systemic stress active, or the energy is wrong in a way you can't yet name. Not today.

What to check: High-impact macro events today or within your expected hold window | VIX environment — spiking or collapsing in ways that distort price behavior | Broader market regime intact, or sector rotation / risk-off shift underway | Does the day feel alive and directional, or heavy and unresolved?

Note: Vedic timing (The Temporal Field) will deepen this layer when integrated. Same slot, same scoring, richer precision about why the vibe is what it is.

The Technical Layers

Layer 2 — Weekly Field State
Is the market putting itself out there?

The market's dominant posture at full scale. Read the weekly chart and chaos metrics together.

Sovereign +2

Healthy DOM. Directed, available, moving with intention. FD 1.2–1.45, LE negative.

The Smush 0

Transitional. Present but not yet offering itself. Energy gathering. FD >1.6, LE flipping. This is often where setups live — not a negative state, just not ready yet.

Tyrant STOP

Unhealthy DOM. Performing power it doesn't actually have. Forced, brittle, parabolic. FD 1.5–1.6, LE weakening. No swing setup exists in a weekly Tyrant. Wait for resolution.

Layer 3 — Daily Field State
Is the pull real?

Present-moment temperature check. Given the weekly backdrop — is the daily confirming? This is a single read of today's quality.

Sovereign +1

Real pull, building. Daily confirming the weekly.

The Smush +1

Heat present, still coiling. Daily Smush within a weekly Sovereign is a setup condition — the consolidation before extension. This earns +1 not because Smush is good but because at this timeframe, given a DOM weekly backdrop, it's often where entries live.

Tyrant -1

Scattered, forced, erratic. Daily energy degrading the weekly signal.

Note: Daily Smush scores +1 while Weekly Smush scores 0. This asymmetry is intentional — the +1 is contextual to its position in the timeframe hierarchy, not inherent to Smush itself.

Layer 4 — Timeframe Convergence
Is all of you pulling?

Not a temperature read — a listening. Are every scale and register oriented the same direction simultaneously? You can have real daily desire (Layer 3 hot) with weekly ambivalence (Layer 4 fractured). That specific combination — surface heat without systemic agreement — is a named condition. Reduce size or wait.

Full Convergence +2

Every timeframe saying the same thing. Weekly, daily, entry TF all pointing the same direction with the same energy.

Partial Accord +1

Mostly aligned. One register hesitating. Proceed at reduced size.

Fractured 0

Conflicting signals at different scales. Something in the system hasn't decided. The body may want this but the system is split.

Layer 5 — Signal Integrity
Is there substance here?

How deep does this actually go? Measured by Hurst Exponent persistence and overall chaos metric coherence.

High Conviction +1

Genuine Hurst persistence. H >0.8 (post-recalibration). Full-body wanting beneath the move.

Strong Signal +1

Solid and directional. H 0.6–0.8 (post-recalibration). Present and real.

Moderate 0

Something here but not deep. Informational only.

Noise -1

Surface agitation without underlying movement. H <0.5. The market is making noise, not a move.

Note: Strength Index thresholds (>0.8, 0.6–0.8) require recalibration against known A+ setups using the corrected indicator. Currently treat Signal Integrity as informational — not determinative — until recalibration is complete.

Layer 6 — Pattern Clarity
Do you have somewhere to go?

Is there a specific destination — a cuff level where the market has shown its nature before? Not just "there's a level" but what kind of location is this?

Clean Signal +1 Steel Cuff

Deepest imprint. Returned here multiple times, held, pushed, held again. This is a reunion with someone who already knows exactly what you want. Highest conviction destination.

Readable +1 Leather Cuff

History and grip. Tested meaningfully. Something established here. Real but not the deepest level.

The Void 0 Velvet Cuff or None

Soft, first-meeting energy — the market has been here but gently — or no clear destination visible. Proceed only if all other layers are strong.

The Somatic Layers

Layer 7 — The Instrument
Hell yes or not right now?

The body check before committing. This is the primary instrument. Not excitement — ease. The clean signal baseline is calm and settled, not activated.

Calm Ease +2

Belly warmth, openness, absence of urgency. Clean yes. This is what you're calibrated against from the Intention Ritual.

Neutral 0

Inconclusive. Something is unresolved. Proceed at reduced size with full attention.

Solar Plexus Alarm STOP

Hard stop. Tightness, anxiety, urgency, wrongness in the gut. Your documented history shows this signal outperforms every analytical override attempted. Do not trade.

Critical context: The framework was built during a period of systemic somatic invalidation. It has never been tested in its own optimal conditions. When trading resumes it is a genuinely new experiment.

Layer 8 — Instrument Agreement
Are you both saying the same thing?

Final check. Body and chart — same market? This is the heaviest single flag in the system if it fires.

Aligned +1

Two channels of perception arriving at the same truth simultaneously. Body and chart speaking the same language is confirmatory information — not merely neutral.

Conflicted -2

Something is split. The body and the chart are not in the same trade. This is an active red flag, not a neutral state. The -2 is the most significant single penalty in the scoring system. Wait until they speak together.
